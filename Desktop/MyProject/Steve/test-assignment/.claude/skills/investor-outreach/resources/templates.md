[Optional: Brief personal connection or reason for reaching out]

We've [traction milestone]:
- [Metric 1]: [Number]
- [Metric 2]: [Number]
- [Metric 3]: [Number]

[Your Name]

[Your Name]

### Follow-Up Template 3 (Final Check-In)

**Subject**: Closing the loop

I'll take this as a not right now - no worries!

If your interest changes or you'd like to stay in the loop, let me know. Otherwise, I'll keep you updated on our major milestones.

Wishing you the best,
[Your Name]

## Best Practices

### Email Structure

1. **Subject Line**
   - Keep it under 50 characters
   - Include company name
   - Make it specific and clear
   - Examples:
     - Good: "Acme Corp - AI-powered sales automation"
     - Bad: "Quick question" or "Meeting?"

2. **Opening**
   - Personalize when possible
   - Reference mutual connections
   - Show you've done research
   - Keep it under 2 sentences

3. **Value Proposition**
   - One-line pitch
   - 3 bullet points of traction
   - Specific metrics (not vague claims)
   - Social proof (team, customers, investors)

4. **Call to Action**
   - Clear and specific
   - Low friction (ask for call, not commitment)
   - Easy to say yes
   - Examples:
     - Good: "Are you open to a 15-min call next week?"
     - Bad: "Can I have $1M?" or "Let me know if you're interested"

### Timing

- **Send times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM local time
- **Follow-up cadence**:
  - Initial email
  - Follow-up 1: 3-4 days later
  - Follow-up 2: 1 week later
  - Final check-in: 1 week later
- **Respect silence**: After 3-4 touches, move on

### Common Mistakes

❌ **Don't**:
- Send generic mass emails
- Make it all about you (focus on investor's interests)
- Be vague or use buzzwords
- Send walls of text
- Forget to proofread (typos kill credibility)
- Be pushy or aggressive
- Ignore investor's focus areas

✅ **Do**:
- Research each investor personally
- Highlight relevant portfolio companies
- Show traction with specific numbers
- Keep it concise (under 200 words)
- Proofread multiple times
- Be professional and respectful
- Follow up respectfully
