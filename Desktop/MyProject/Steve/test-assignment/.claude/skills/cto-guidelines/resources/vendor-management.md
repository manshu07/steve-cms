## Vendor Types

### SaaS Tools
- Monthly/annual licensing
- Quick deployment
- Vendor-managed infrastructure

### Cloud Services
- Usage-based pricing
- Scalable infrastructure
- Multiple service tiers

### Professional Services
- Project-based or retainer
- Specialized expertise
- Time-limited engagements

## Contract Management

### Key Contract Terms
- **Term:** 1-3 years typical
- **Price Lock:** Negotiate annual increases
- **Exit Clause:** 30-90 days notice
- **Data Ownership:** We own our data
- **SLA:** 99.9% uptime minimum
- **Security:** SOC 2 compliance required

### Renewal Checklist
- [ ] Usage review
- [ ] Price comparison with alternatives
- [ ] Feature needs assessment
- [ ] Contract renegotiation
- [ ] Stakeholder approval

## Cost Optimization

### Regular Reviews
- Monthly usage reports
- Quarterly cost reviews
- Annual contract audits

### Cost Reduction Strategies
1. **Consolidate tools** - Reduce overlapping services
2. **Negotiate volume discounts** - Bundle services
3. **Right-size usage** - Match plan to actual usage
4. **Annual prepay** - Often 10-20% discount
